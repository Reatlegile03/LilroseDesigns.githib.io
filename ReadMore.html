Lilrose designs started in 2021.The founder of the business,
known as Elizabeth Tshabalala is a self taught designer.She grew up having the love for fashion,
from a toddler stage she used to hand stich dresses for dolls.In 2021,that's when she bought
her first sewing machine and upgraded and perfected her sewing skills from watching YouTube
tutorials.She started with designing clothes for herself then later her work got exposed
to people and started receiving orders.


The challenges she has encountered,was not having enough capital to get other
equipments she needed for the business and also not having enough working space. but
she is finally working on that. Her vision in this fashion industry,is to tell a story
through fashion,and be a global competitive business. Her mission is be creative and
innovative designer as per customers satisfaction.
